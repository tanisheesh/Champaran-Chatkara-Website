# **App Name**: Champaran Chatkara

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Menu Display: Present food categories in interactive tabs (e.g., 'Veg', 'Non-Veg'), allowing users to browse items with descriptions and prices.
- Signature Dishes Section: A dedicated area to highlight and showcase the restaurant's best-selling and most popular dishes.
- About Us Story: A compelling section providing background, mission, and the unique story of Champaran Chatkara.
- Location & Contact Details: Clearly display restaurant address, operating hours, contact numbers, and an embedded map for directions.
- Admin Content Management Panel: A secure panel for restaurant staff to manage menu items, prices, descriptions, best-seller designations, and other website content.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A rich, warm ochre that evokes the spices and depth of traditional Indian cuisine (#D48311).
- Background color: A subtly warm, very light creamy off-white that provides a clean canvas and soft contrast (#F7F5F3).
- Accent color: A vibrant, deep red to draw attention to key elements and complement the warm primary hue (#D93526).
- Headlines font: 'Literata' (transitional serif) for a touch of authenticity and warmth, suitable for an establishment with a story.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for modern clarity, legibility, and a friendly tone across descriptions and information.
- Use clean, illustrative icons related to food, dining, and contact details, maintaining a coherent style.
- A spacious, image-centric layout that highlights delicious food photography, with intuitive navigation tabs for menu categories.
- Subtle hover animations on menu items and smooth transitions between menu tabs to enhance user engagement without distraction.
